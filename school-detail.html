Writing Tips for UAB
Expert advice for this specific school
🏛️
Question 1 — Alabama Ties: Even if you have no direct ties, you can mention your interest in serving underserved populations in the state, UAB's mission, or clinical programs that align with your goals. No ties is acceptable — be honest.
🎯
Question 2 — Why UAB: Research UAB's specific programs, clinics, and curriculum. Mention the D4 community outreach program, their research opportunities, or faculty whose work aligns with your interests. Avoid generic answers.
🩺
Question 3 — Healthcare Exposure: Be specific about hours, settings, and what you observed or contributed. Don't just list activities — describe what you learned and how it confirmed your commitment to dentistry.
⏱️
No Word Limit Strategy: Aim for 250–400 words per question. "No limit" doesn't mean write a novel — admissions committees appreciate clarity and conciseness. Structure each response with a clear opening, body, and takeaway.
📅
Submit Early: UAB reviews on a rolling basis. Submitting within 2 weeks of receiving your secondary dramatically improves your chances of an interview invite.
